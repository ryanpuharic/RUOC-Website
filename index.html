Rutgers University Outdoors Club

About

We are Rutgers University's student run outdoors club! We have trips involving all sorts of outdoor activities, including hiking, backpacking, rock climbing, fishing, kayaking, snowshoeing, whitewater rafting, canoeing, caving, and even more. Our trips range in difficulty- some are great for beginners, some are better for more experienced people, and many are somewhere in between!
